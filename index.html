About Us

In the bustling streets of Pune, a crisis gripped the hearts of animal lovers as rising dog food costs made it harder to feed the city’s street dogs. Volunteers faced the heartbreaking choice between feeding more dogs or providing better nutrition. Out of this struggle, Barkfeast was born—a movement committed to ensuring no dog goes hungry or is fed substandard food due to circumstances beyond anyone’s control. Founded by individuals who have walked in the shoes of those tirelessly caring for stray animals, Barkfeast is a beacon of hope for both the dogs and the people who love them.

Barkfeast’s mission is simple yet profound: to provide high-quality, affordable dog food that nourishes not only the bodies of street dogs but also the spirits of the volunteers who dedicate themselves to their care. By keeping prices accessible without compromising on quality, Barkfeast aims to empower animal shelters and street dog feeders, creating a ripple effect of kindness across communities. It stands as a promise that no one feeding a hungry dog will have to sacrifice nutrition for cost, fostering a world where every wagging tail is a testament to compassion.

As Barkfeast looks toward expansion, its vision extends beyond Pune to cities and towns nationwide. Carrying the stories of volunteers, compassionate children, and countless street dogs, Barkfeast is more than just a brand—it is a movement driven by empathy and action. With a focus on making a lasting impact, Barkfeast is dedicated to creating a future where no stray goes hungry and every act of kindness leaves a lasting mark.

Our Mission

At the heart of Barkfeast lies a mission that burns bright with compassion and determination. We envision a world where the sight of a malnourished street dog is a thing of the past, where every canine life is valued, nurtured, and celebrated. Our mission goes beyond mere sustenance - we aim to create an ecosystem that transforms how society perceives and treats its most vulnerable four-legged members.

We are commited to

#1

Community Care

The streets of our cities are not just thoroughfares; they are home to countless loyal companions who ask for nothing more than a kind word and a decent meal. By empowering local communities to care for their street dogs, we're not just feeding animals - we're nurturing the very soul of our neighbourhoods. Every bowl of Barkfeast served is a step towards a more compassionate society.

#2

Animal Welfare

The wagging tail of a well-fed dog is music to our ears. By making nutritious food affordable, we're composing a symphony of health and happiness that resonates through every street and alley. Our goal is to see street dogs not just surviving, but thriving - their coats shiny, their eyes bright, and their spirits unbroken.

#3

Access to Nutrition

In a world of abundance, it's a tragedy that so many dogs go hungry. Barkfeast is our way of tipping the scales, ensuring that quality nutrition is not a luxury but a basic right for every dog, regardless of their circumstance. We believe that every dog deserves to know the joy of a full belly and the energy to play and love.

#4

Reducing Shelter Burden

Animal shelters are often the last line of defence for street dogs, but they're buckling under the weight of overwhelming need. By providing affordable, nutritious food, we aim to keep more dogs healthy on the streets, allowing shelters to focus their limited resources on the most vulnerable cases. Every dog we help keep out of a shelter is a victory for both the dog and the tireless shelter workers.

#5

Community Engagement

We don't just sell dog food; we create an ecosystem by making it easier for people to feed street dogs, we're creating an army of everyday heroes. From the college student who feeds the campus strays to the office worker who keeps a bag of Barkfeast in their car, we're empowering people to make a tangible difference in their communities without making a hole in their pocket.

#6

Social Responsibility

At Barkfeast, we believe that the measure of a society is how it treats its most vulnerable members - human and animal alike. By addressing the needs of street dogs, we're holding up a mirror to our own humanity and challenging ourselves to be better. Our commitment to ethical practices and community welfare is not just a business strategy - it's the very foundation of who we are.
